Our Classes

From high-intensity to mindful movement, find the class that fits your goals.

Strength Training

Build muscle and increase power with structured resistance training. Progressive overload programs for all experience levels.

60 min 500-700 cal All Levels

HIIT

High-intensity interval training that torches calories and boosts metabolism. Short bursts of all-out effort with brief recovery.

45 min 600-900 cal Intermediate

Yoga

Improve flexibility, balance, and mental clarity. Vinyasa and power yoga flows designed to complement your training.

60 min 200-400 cal All Levels

Boxing

Learn technique while getting an incredible full-body workout. Heavy bag rounds, pad work, and conditioning drills.

Spin

Indoor cycling with energizing music and coached intervals. Build endurance and leg strength in a motivating group setting.
