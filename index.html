How It Works

From first call to launch day.

A simple, fully remote process built for busy Miami restaurants, salons, and gyms.

01

Quick Intake

You tell us about your business in a few minutes: your name, location, current website or Instagram, and what you want the site to do. We review everything on our side and come back with a clear plan and quote.

100% remote, no in-person visit required
02

Content & Visuals

You send us photos, logos, menus, service lists, and any inspo via a shared folder. If you don't have great photos yet, we help you plan what to shoot with your phone so the site still looks premium.

Everything handled over email and shared drive
03

Design & Build

We design and build a custom site around your brand — no templates. You preview the full site in a private link, request edits, and we refine until it feels exactly like your space.

Concepts within a week, typical turnaround 2–3 weeks
04

Launch & Ongoing Support

We handle the domain, hosting setup, and technical details. Your site goes live, and we stay on for tweaks, updates, and making sure everything runs smooth.
