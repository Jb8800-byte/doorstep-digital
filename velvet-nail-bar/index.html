About Velvet

A studio built on care, not just polish.

Velvet Nail Bar was created for people who want more from a nail appointment. Not louder music and faster turnover, but a calm, intentional space where the work speaks for itself.

We use exclusively non-toxic, cruelty-free products. Our technicians are trained artists, not just applicators. Every station is sanitized between clients, every tool is hospital-grade sterilized, and every visit starts and ends on your terms.
